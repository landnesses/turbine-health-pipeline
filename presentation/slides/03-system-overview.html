Overall System Concept

Three coordinated flows form one integrated decision-support architecture.

The system is designed as a coordinated workflow rather than separate AI components. Operational diagnostics, simulation advisory, and human review are connected end to end.

01

Operational data flow

SCADA data is processed through anomaly extraction, health metadata, and explanation to produce operator-ready briefings.

02

Simulation advisory flow

Reinforcement learning explores strategies in simulation and provides optimization advice as decision support.

03

Human-in-the-loop flow

Operators review AI outputs before operational adoption, maintaining engineering accountability.

04

Key principle

AI supports turbine operations but does not directly control turbine hardware in this prototype.

TakeawayThe architecture combines diagnosis, advisory optimization, and human governance in one system.
