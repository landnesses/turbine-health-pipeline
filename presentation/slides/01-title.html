Core Message

The system does not let AI directly control a real turbine. Instead, it converts SCADA evidence into interpretable health signals, uses a language model to explain maintenance implications, and only then decides whether RL optimization advice is allowed.

Presentation Thesis

Real-world SCADA signals are hard to interpret quickly.

Operators need both diagnosis support and operational guidance.

Optimization should never bypass maintenance and safety context.

TakeawayThe system unifies diagnosis, explanation, and optimization advisory in one coherent operator workflow.
