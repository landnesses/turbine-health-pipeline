Reinforcement Learning Advisory Module

Simulation-backed optimization guidance under explicit safety constraints.

The RL component explores operation strategies in simulation and balances power capture, structural load reduction, and control smoothness. RL is included as evidence-backed advisory logic.

Optimization objectives
  • Power capture under changing wind conditions.
  • Structural load reduction for safer operation.
  • Control smoothness to avoid aggressive control actions.
RL does not control real turbines. It provides advisory optimization guidance filtered by the health gate.
Important constraint

Simulation exploration is useful because risky strategies can be tested before any operational adoption.
