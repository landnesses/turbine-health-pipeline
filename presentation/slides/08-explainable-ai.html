Explainable and Controllable AI Design

Interpretable AI behavior with explicit operational governance.

A key design objective is that recommendations remain explainable to engineers and controllable by operational safety policies.

Explainability mechanisms
  • Interpretable health metadata as explicit evidence.
  • Rule-based safety gating with visible conditions.
  • Transparent optimization objectives and reward design.
Each feature maps to measurable turbine properties.
Controllability mechanisms
  • Health-state gating of optimization recommendations.
  • Maintenance status can override optimization advice.
  • Human operator remains the final authority.
System philosophy: AI assists operators rather than replacing them.
TakeawayExplainability and controllability are built into the workflow, not added afterward.
