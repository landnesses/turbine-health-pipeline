Problem & Motivation

The operational gap is not data collection. It is decision conversion.

Wind turbines already generate rich SCADA telemetry, but operators still need to bridge a difficult step: from abnormal signals to explainable, safe, and timely action.

Current reality
  • SCADA platforms collect alarms, statuses, and operating trends continuously.
  • Anomaly detection often ends at highlighting unusual rows or codes.
  • Operators still need to interpret risk, maintenance priority, and action urgency manually.
Diagnosis exists, but operator-ready decision support is often fragmented.
Project motivation
  • Turn noisy telemetry into turbine-day health evidence.
  • Explain maintenance implications in natural language.
  • Only expose optimisation advice under explicit safety constraints.
The goal is to provide a unified engineering workflow, not just raw anomaly scores.
TakeawayThe project closes the gap between SCADA anomaly detection and actionable operator support.
